## Primary Sources (Government / Official)

### Arizona Department of Water Resources (ADWR)
- URL: https://azwater.gov
- Type: Primary — state government
- Key data: Groundwater basin maps, AMA boundaries, annual reports, assured water supply
- Accessed: —
- Notes: Main regulatory body for AZ water

### Central Arizona Project (CAP)
- URL: https://www.cap-az.com
- Type: Primary — water authority
- Key data: CAP canal operations, allocation data, water delivery reports
- Accessed: —
- Notes: Delivers Colorado River water to central and southern AZ

### Salt River Project (SRP)
- URL: https://www.srpnet.com
- Type: Primary — water and power utility
- Key data: Reservoir levels, water supply reports, service area maps
- Accessed: —
- Notes: Manages Salt and Verde River water for Phoenix metro

### City of Phoenix Water Services
- URL: https://www.phoenix.gov/waterservices
- Type: Primary — municipal utility
- Key data: Water quality reports, supply mix, infrastructure
- Accessed: —
- Notes: Primary water utility for Phoenix

### US Bureau of Reclamation (USBR)
- URL: https://www.usbr.gov
- Type: Primary — federal government
- Key data: Lake Mead levels, Colorado River allocations, shortage declarations
- Accessed: —
- Notes: Manages federal water infrastructure including Hoover Dam

### USGS Water Resources
- URL: https://water.usgs.gov
- Type: Primary — federal science agency
- Key data: Real-time stream gauges, groundwater levels, water use data
- Accessed: —
- Notes: Best source for raw hydrological data
