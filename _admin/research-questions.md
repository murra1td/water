# Research Questions

> These are the questions this project is trying to answer. Every source and research note maps back to one of these.

---

## Big Picture

- [ ] What are Arizona's primary water sources and what percentage comes from each?
- [ ] How does Arizona's water supply compare to its current and projected demand?
- [ ] What is the legal framework governing Arizona water rights (prior appropriation, AMA zones)?
- [ ] How does the Colorado River Compact affect Arizona's allocation?

## Infrastructure

- [ ] What is the Central Arizona Project (CAP) and how does it work?
- [ ] What is the Salt River Project (SRP) and how does it differ from CAP?
- [ ] How does water get from the Colorado River to a Phoenix tap?
- [ ] How many treatment plants serve the Phoenix metro and where are they?
- [ ] What is the distribution network — canals, pipes, pressure zones?

## Source to Tap (Phoenix Specific)

- [ ] What is Phoenix's water supply mix today (CAP / SRP / groundwater / reclaimed)?
- [ ] Which reservoirs feed Phoenix's system?
- [ ] What treatment process does Phoenix water go through?
- [ ] How does reclaimed water fit into the system?
- [ ] Can I trace a specific address back to its water source?

## Stress & Vulnerability

- [ ] What happens to Phoenix's water supply if CAP allocations are cut further?
- [ ] What is the current level of Lake Mead and what are the trigger thresholds?
- [ ] How much groundwater is Arizona overdrafting annually?
- [ ] What is Arizona doing to prepare for reduced Colorado River flows?

## Data & Tool

- [ ] Is there a public dataset mapping water utilities to addresses?
- [ ] Does Phoenix Water publish GIS data on its service area and infrastructure?
- [ ] What data would be needed to build an address → water source lookup tool?
